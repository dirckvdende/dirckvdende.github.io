For this puzzle you need to place the chess pieces on the squares such that no piece can attack another. Pieces (specifically the rook, bishop, and queen) cannot attack other pieces by over spots outside the play area. This means that **(A)** is allowed, but **(B)** is not.

![Puzzle image](/assets/puzzles/3/examples.png)
